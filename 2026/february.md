# February 2026

## Version 2026.68.0 — February 5, 2026
**Release Date:** February 5, 2026 – 1:00 AM PST

### New Functionality
**FT-2558: Encounter Workflow Templates.** Care teams can now create reusable encounter templates that pre-populate assessment forms, task lists, and documentation prompts for common visit types, reducing setup time and ensuring consistency.

**FT-2561: Patient Communication History View.** A unified communication timeline now displays all SMS, email, and in-app messages sent to or received from a patient in a single chronological view, giving care teams full context at a glance.

### Enhancements
**FT-2564: Improved Calendar Conflict Detection.** Scheduling now surfaces conflicts in real time when booking appointments, including overlapping encounters and unavailable provider time slots, before the appointment is saved.

## Version 2026.70.0 — February 19, 2026
**Release Date:** February 19, 2026 – 1:00 AM PST

### New Functionality
**FT-2567: Role-Based Dashboard Configuration.** Administrators can define default dashboard layouts per user role, ensuring that care coordinators, nurses, and physicians each see the most relevant widgets and metrics when they log in.

**FT-2570: Automated Outreach Campaigns.** Organizations can configure multi-step outreach sequences triggered by patient events—such as enrollment, missed appointments, or assessment completion—to automatically send reminders and follow-ups via SMS or email.

### Enhancements
**FT-2572: Assessment Scoring Improvements.** Scored assessments now support weighted questions and configurable score thresholds, with color-coded risk indicators displayed directly on the patient chart for faster clinical triage.

**FT-2574: Worklist Filter Persistence.** Filter and sort preferences on worklists are now saved per user, so care team members no longer need to reapply their preferred view each session.
