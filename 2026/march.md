# March 2026

## Version 2026.71.0 — March 5, 2026
**Release Date:** March 5, 2026 – 1:00 AM PST

### New Functionality
**FT-2575: Question Groups in Assessments.** Assessments now support grouped question sections with collapsible headers, allowing clinicians to organize complex assessments into logical categories and improve readability for long-form clinical evaluations.

### Enhancements
**FT-2577: Bulk Export of Patient Records.** Administrators can now initiate bulk exports of patient records in CSV or PDF format for reporting and compliance purposes, with configurable field selection and date range filters.

## Version 2026.71.0 — March 19, 2026
**Release Date:** March 19, 2026 – 1:00 AM PDT

### New Functionality
**FT-2580: Care Plan Goal Tracking.** Care plans now include structured goal-tracking with milestone dates, completion status, and progress notes, giving care teams and patients a shared view of treatment objectives and outcomes.

**FT-2582: Automated Prior Authorization Alerts.** Welkin can now surface prior authorization requirements based on patient insurance and ordered services, prompting care teams to initiate PA workflows before scheduling to reduce claim denials.

### Enhancements
**FT-2585: Improved Notification Preferences.** Users can now configure granular notification preferences by event type and delivery channel (in-app, email, or SMS), reducing notification fatigue and ensuring alerts reach the right people the right way.

**FT-2587: Performance Improvements to Patient Search.** Patient search results now load significantly faster for organizations with large patient populations, with improved indexing and relevance ranking for partial name and MRN lookups.
